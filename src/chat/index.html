Chat

Open channel on libera.chat — #wuld-ink. Pick a nickname, click Start, talk. No account required for read-along; if you want to keep the same handle across visits, register it with NickServ once and it is yours.

House rules — informal

Disagreement is welcome. Bad faith is not. Trolling, slurs, harassment, and grooming attempts are removed without explanation. Spam vanishes on sight.

This is not a support channel and not a therapy channel. If you are in crisis, please call or text 988 (US Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) or your local equivalent.
