The Void Engine — Gallery

Exhibition handout in the form of a wall. Black-and-white and monochrome output; analog-decay register. The wall text refuses the conventions of wall text — no invitation to contemplate, no biography, no consolation. The images were not made to be understood. They were made to be looked at until looking becomes uncomfortable, and then a little longer. Currently: plates I–XXVII, batches 01–02.
