13 · Support

Support

Nothing here is paywalled, and nothing here will ever be. If you contribute, it is because you want the umbrella to keep existing — the domain to remain renewed, the storage to keep returning bytes, the work to keep being produced under conditions that do not require it to perform for anyone. No tiered rewards. No exclusive content. No thank-you note unless you write first and ask for one.

01 · Recurring or one-time

Donate via PayPal

One button. Pick any amount at PayPal’s checkout; tick the “monthly” option if you want it to recur. Suggested defaults $3 / $5 / $10 / $100, all adjustable. Cancel a recurring donation any time from your PayPal account.

Where it goes

Cloudflare registrar renewal ($20/yr), R2 storage class fees (a few dollars per month at current bandwidth), occasional one-off costs for fonts, audio mastering, or back-end services. Anything beyond running-costs funds time spent producing the work the umbrella exists to hold. No advertising, no sponsorships, no algorithmic optimisation. If the costs are ever recouped in a given month, the surplus carries forward against months they are not.

Disclaimer

Donations are voluntary contributions to support the umbrella’s ongoing operating costs and the author’s time. They are not transactions for goods or services, not subject to refund as such, and do not establish a paid-supporter tier, reward structure, or contractual obligation. The umbrella claims no tax-exempt status; donations are not tax-deductible. Tax treatment in your jurisdiction is your affair. Routing platforms (PayPal, CashApp, Venmo) impose their own fees + terms; what reaches the umbrella is what each platform releases after their cut.
