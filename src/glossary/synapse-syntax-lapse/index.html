synapse-syntax-lapse

Pos. n. Etymology synapse + syntax + lapse First use WULD editorial canon (broader corpus)

Definition

The neuron-misfire that occurs when consciousness attempts to comprehend non-feeling-nothingness. “The expression of expressionless”: non-founded, no-thing, no image, no place, no names.

Etymology & register

Triadic compound: synapse (the neurological substrate of cognition) + syntax (the formal-linguistic structure cognition depends on) + lapse (a failure or gap). The compound names a failure-point where neurological substrate fails to support formal-linguistic continuity. Typographic intent: intentionally lowercase and hyphenated, pattern-matching w-holes. Capitalize only at sentence-start.
